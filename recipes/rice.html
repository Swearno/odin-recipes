Rice

Description

This freshly cooked rice is an amazing dish to have not only as a side but as a main meal. Added with a touch of butter and a pinch of cracked black pepper to make this rice taste even better. You've never had rice like this before so why not learn the recipe and try it out yourself.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Rice
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Get your uncooked rice and but in in a pot on the stove
  2. Take some water and put it in the pot with the rice
  3. Set stove heat on between 3-5 and let it boil for 10-15 minutes
  4. WHen done let the rice cool for 3-5 minutes
  5. Take out the amount you want in a bowl and add two tsp of butter
  6. Add a pinch of black pepper and enjoy!
