Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe is one that you've never tried before. If you think that this pizza will taste like garbage, your wrong. Starts out with the dough, beating and rolling it until its in a perfect circle shape. Next grab your tomato sauce that was handpicked from Italy and evenly spread it across the dough until it's completely spread.

Then add your mozzarella cheese and nicely spread it across the sauced up dough till you can't see the sauce anymore. Finally pop it in the oven for 20 minutes until crust is golden brown and leave it 2 minutes out of the oven to cool. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Roll out dough until flat
  2. Get tomato sauce and spread it on dough evenly
  3. Get mozzarella cheese and sprinkle it on tomato sauce until fully covered
  4. Put it in the oven for 20 minutes until golden brown
  5. Let pizza cool out of oven for 2-3 minutes until ready to serve
