Chicken Breast

Description

This lovely chicken breast will have your mouth watering for more with each bite. This juicy, freshly cooked chicken has a nice layer of BBQ sauce on top, mixed in with the tasty seasonings that bring out the flavor of the chicken breast to make this the best chicken breast you will ever eat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Seasoning
  • Barbecue Sauce

Steps

  1. Defrost you chicken breast
  2. After defrosting marinate your chicken in some BBQ sauce until fully covered
  3. Next, add the seasonings on top of the chicken breast until its fully covered
  4. Now put the chicken on a pan and in the oven for 20 mins -flip the chicken after 10 minutes-
  5. When done, take out the chicken breast and let it sit for 5 minutes and enjoy!
